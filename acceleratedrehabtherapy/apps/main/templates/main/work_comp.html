There are several types of workplace injuries. In some cases, an individual sustains only minor bumps and bruises. Other times, work-related injuries are life-threatening. For more serious issues, it's not uncommon for physicians to refer patients to a Doctor of Chiropractic. For many years, this was taboo. Today, most physicians recognize the value of chiropractic care.

Common Work-Related Injuries

As far as the types of work-related injuries, these range from mild to severe. The following represent the work-related injuries that benefit the most from rehabilitation:

  • Muscle strains
  • Repetitive strains
  • Sprains or fractures from slips and falls
  • Car crash pain
  • Migraines from inhaling toxic fumes
  • Post-surgical
  • Spinal cord trauma

Our Rehabilitation Approach

At Accelerated Rehab Therapy, we offer four distinct types of rehabilitation, namely, chiropractic care, physical therapy, therapeutic massage, and nutrition counseling. Our No. 1 goal, regardless of the type of work comp injury, is to help patients achieve a full recovery in the fastest time possible.

OVERCOMING WORKERS' COMP INJURIES

Along with chiropractic care, physical therapy, and other treatment options for workers' comp injuries, we address problems that have nothing to do with the job. For instance, our skilled team at Accelerated Rehab Therapy sees a lot of patients with an auto injury. For that reason, we have an actual auto accident plan that streamlines the treatment and billing processes.

We also specialize in work injury cases. Our licenced specialist has seen just about everything. We understand that workers' comp injuries can sometimes pose challenges. Obviously, the patient needs rehabilitation as quickly as possible, but that can mean fighting with the insurance provider.

For workers' comp chiropractic care, our goal is to provide patients with the care they need, but at the same time, we de-escalate stress that comes from dealing with the insurance company.
