Q: What Are Some Signs that I May Have a Whiplash Injury?

A: Signs and symptoms of whiplash usually develop within days of the injury and may include:

  • Neck pain and stiffness
  • Ringing in ears (tinnitus)
  • Loss of range of motion in the neck
  • Headaches, most often starting at the base of the skull
  • Tenderness or pain in the shoulder, upper back, or arms
  • Tingling or numbness in the arms
  • Sleep disturbances
  • Dizziness

Q: How Does Chiropractic Care Relieve Auto Accident Back Pain and Neck Pain?

A: Chiropractic treatment addresses the underlying injury, whether it is spinal or whiplash. Chiropractic adjustments not only can relieve pressure on the muscles and nerves but also brings alignment back to the spine. Additional therapies, such as corrective exercise, can help bring back the body's full range of motion.
