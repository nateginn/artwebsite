Why Heel Pain Sometimes Does Not Improve

Plantar fasciitis can become stubborn when the tissue remains irritated or overloaded for long periods of time. Many patients continue experiencing pain despite trying rest, braces, stretching, or shoe changes.

Shockwave therapy is commonly used as part of a rehabilitation-focused treatment plan to help support the healing process and improve function.
