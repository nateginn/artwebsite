OUR MISSION

At Accelerated Rehab Therapy, our mission is to empower our patients to live life without limitations through personalized, evidence-based rehabilitation care. We combine clinical excellence with compassionate service to help each individual achieve optimal wellness and maintain an active lifestyle.

Through our integrated approach to physical therapy, chiropractic care, massage therapy, and acupuncture, we're committed to accelerating your journey to recovery and long-term health.

OUR CORE VALUES

PATIENT-CENTERED CARE

Every treatment plan is tailored to your specific needs, goals, and lifestyle. We listen, we care, and we adapt our approach to ensure the best possible outcomes for each patient.

CLINICAL EXCELLENCE

Our team maintains the highest standards of professional expertise through continuous education and implementation of evidence-based practices.

COLLABORATIVE APPROACH

We believe in the power of integrated care. Our team works together, combining different therapeutic approaches to provide comprehensive treatment solutions.

WHY CHOOSE A.R.T.?

Comprehensive Care

We offer a full spectrum of rehabilitation services under one roof, including chiropractic care, physical therapy, massage therapy, and acupuncture. This integrated approach ensures you receive the most effective combination of treatments for your condition.

Experienced Team

Our skilled practitioners bring years of experience in treating various conditions, from auto and work injuries to chronic pain and sports-related issues. We're committed to staying current with the latest treatment techniques and technologies.

Convenient Locations

With locations in Greeley, Denver, and at the UNC campus, we make it easy for you to access quality care. Our flexible scheduling and efficient insurance processing help ensure a smooth treatment experience.
